Detective Holmes - The brilliant detective, master of deduction
Dr. Watson - Holmes' loyal friend and chronicler of their adventures
Inspector Lestrade - Scotland Yard detective who often seeks Holmes' help
Mrs. Hudson - Holmes and Watson's landlady at 221B Baker Street
Professor Moriarty - Holmes' arch-nemesis, the Napoleon of Crime
Mycroft Holmes - Sherlock's older brother, government official
Irene Adler - The woman who outsmarted Holmes
Colonel Moran - Moriarty's second in command
Mary Morstan - Watson's wife, former client
Wiggins - Leader of the Baker Street Irregulars
